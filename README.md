## E-Commerce Customer Churn Analysis
End-to-end customer churn analysis using Python (Pandas) and Power BI. Uncovered actionable pricing and volume insights across 2,000 subscriber accounts to reduce customer attrition and protect recurring revenue.

## 🎯 Executive Summary
This project analyzes an e-commerce operational dataset containing 2,000 customer accounts to identify the primary behavioral drivers of subscription cancellations. By engineering data workflows across Python and Power BI, I discovered that product pricing structures—rather than customer service bottlenecks—serve as the primary leading indicator for total subscriber churn.

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack
* Exploratory Data Analysis: Python (Pandas)
* Data Visualization: Seaborn & Matplotlib
* Business Intelligence Dashboard: Power BI Desktop

## 🔍 Key Strategic Insights
* The Baseline Problem: The platform is experiencing a total subscriber churn rate of 24.65% , resulting in a total revenue loss of $649.44K.
* The Price Sensitivity Trap: Churned users buy significantly more expensive individual items on average than active users. High premium entry costs are driving account cancellations.
* The Bulk Purchase Retention Path: Active, loyal subscribers prefer predictable bulk value purchasing, maintaining a higher average items-per-order count at a lower unit price point.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

   1. Implement a Tiered Subscription Model: Offer bulk discount subscription structures to incentivize high-unit item purchases, aligning with active user behavior.
   2. Automate Price-Point Risk Triggers: Create automated email marketing sequences or milestone discount check-ins for users whose average cart value exceeds premium thresholds, mitigating high-price attrition before cancellation occurs.
